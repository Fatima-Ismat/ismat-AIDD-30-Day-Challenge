Mere liye AI-Driven Development ka matlab hai ke coding ka pura tareeqa change ho gaya hai. Ab developer sirf code likhne wala insan nahi rahe, balke AI k guide ban gaye hain. AI ab repetitive kaam karta hai, code suggest karta hai, debugging me help deta hai, aur developer ka time logic aur planning par lagta hai. AIDD mujhe yeh feel karata hai ke ab development fast, smarter aur zyada creative ho gayi hai.
